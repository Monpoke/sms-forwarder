SMS Forwarder

What's this app?

This app is used for listening your SMSs and forwarding to a server.

How to use

Just run it, and keep it running in background (make sure that you don't kill it).

How to use modify it?

Take a look at the src/app/app.component.ts file, update apiEndpoint variable for your api end-point.

Disclaimer

This app need permission to access your SMS which can violate the privacy of users. Please consider for using in commerce.
